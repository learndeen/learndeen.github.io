Complete Guide to Hajj

~ Based on Sunnah ~

1. Acceptance Conditions

For any act of worship (Hajj included) to be accepted, it needs:

  • Sincerity (Ikhlas): Seeking only Allah's pleasure, not showing off.
  • Following Sunnah: Doing it exactly as the Prophet Ô∑∫ did.

2. The Three Types

1. Tamattu' (Best) ‚≠ê

Ihram for Umrah in Hajj months. Do Umrah → Exit Ihram → Enter Hajj Ihram on 8th Dhul-Hijjah.

*Requires Sacrifice (Hadiy)

2. Qiran

Ihram for Umrah & Hajj together. Do not exit Ihram until Eid day.

*Requires Sacrifice (Hadiy)

3. Ifrad

Ihram for Hajj only. Remain in Ihram until Eid.

*No Sacrifice needed

Note: If doing Qiran/Ifrad but don't have a sacrifice animal, change intention to Umrah (make it Tamattu').

3. The Sacred Journey

Step 1: Ihram

  • Prep: Ghusl, Perfume (body only), Pray.
  • Say Intention:
    • Tamattu': "Labbayk Allahumma bi 'Umrah"
    • Qiran: "Labbayk Allahumma bi Hajjah wa 'Umrah"
    • Ifrad: "Labbayk Allahumma Hajjan"
  • Condition (if fearing obstacles): "If I am prevented, then my exiting Ihram is where I am prevented."
  • The Talbiyah:
    "Labbayka Allahumma labbayk, labbayka la sharika laka labbayk. Inna al-hamd wa'l-ni'mata laka wa'l-mulk, la sharika lak"

Step 2: Entering Makkah

Ghusl before entering (Sunnah). Enter Masjid right foot first.

"Allahumma ighfir li dhunubi waftah li abwab rahmatika..."

Umrah Rituals (For Tamattu'):

  1. Tawaf: 7 circuits around Kaaba.
  2. Sa'i: 7 times Safa ↔ Marwah.
  3. Shave/Cut: Men shave (better) or cut entire head. Women cut fingertip length.
  4. Exit Ihram: Everything becomes lawful (clothes, perfume, etc) until 8th Dhul-Hijjah.

8th Dhul-Hijjah (Tarwiyah)

Action: Enter Hajj Ihram from Makkah ("Labbayk Allahumma Hajjan").

Move to Mina: Pray Zuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, Fajr.

Rule: Shorten prayers (4->2) but DO NOT combine.

9th: Arafah (The Great Day)

  • Morning: Go to Namirah (Sunnah) till noon.
  • Prayer: At Zuhr time, pray Zuhr & Asr combined and shortened.
  • Wuquf (Standing): From noon till Sunset. Face Qiblah (not mountain). Make Dua!
  • Best Dua:
    "La ilaha ill-Allah wahdahu la sharika lah, lahu'l-mulk, wa lahu'l-hamd, wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadir"
üåå

Night of 9th: Muzdalifah

Leave Arafah after sunset.

  • Pray Maghrib (3) & Isha (2) combined upon arrival.
  • Sleep/Rest.
  • Pray Fajr early.
  • Stand at Al-Mash'ar Al-Haram making Dua until "very light" (before sunrise).
üêè

10th: Yawm al-Nahr (Eid)

Move to Mina via Wadi Mahsar. Order of actions:

  • 1. Stone Jamrat al-Aqabah (7 pebbles). Say "Allahu Akbar". Stop Talbiyah.
  • 2. Slaughter Sacrifice (Hadiy).
  • 3. Shave Head (Men) / Trim (Women).
First Tahallul: Everything allowed except marital relations. Perfume is Sunnah now!
  • 4. Go to Makkah: Tawaf al-Ifadah & Sa'i.
Second Tahallul: Everything is now lawful.

11th, 12th, (13th): Tashreeq

Stay in Mina. Stone after Zenith (noon) each day.

  1. Small Jamrah: 7 pebbles → Long Dua.
  2. Middle Jamrah: 7 pebbles → Long Dua.
  3. Aqabah Jamrah: 7 pebbles → NO Dua.

Can leave on 12th if departed before sunset. Otherwise, stay for 13th.

Farewell Tawaf (Wada')

The very last action before leaving Makkah.

Note: Menstruating women are exempt and do not need to stand by the door.

üö´ Prohibitions of Ihram üö´

Everyone:

  • Cutting hair or nails.
  • Perfume (body, clothes, soap, food).
  • Hunting.
  • Marriage contracts/proposals.
  • Sexual intercourse/foreplay (invalidates Hajj if before 1st Tahallul).
  • Wearing gloves.

Men Only:

  • Covering head with attached object (hats/turbans). (Umbrellas/Tents OK)
  • Form-fitting/stitched clothes (Shirts, pants, underwear).
  • Slippers covering heels (unless no sandals available).

Women Only:

  • Niqab or Burqa. (Must cover face with other cloth if men present).
Allowed ‚úÖ: Sandals, Rings, Eyeglasses, Hearing aids, Watches (wrist/neck), Money belts, Scratching/Washing (unscented).

General Requirements

  • Adhere to religious laws (praying on time).
  • Avoid obscene speech and sin (No arguing!).
  • Avoid annoying other Muslims.
